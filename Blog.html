Matter4478 introduces Project_GeoTrack, an easy-to-use fully fledged adventure tracker, to help you go beyond

During the first lockdown (februari-march 2020) it occurred to me that I did not like the way Strava or its competitors were designed. Do not get me wrong, Strava is a great app with lots of features, if you are willing to glance over some privacy issues, but it is not what I imagined an adventure tracker could be.

It matters not who goes the fastest, stays on it the longest or endures the most. The adventure does. It starts with setting goals and training hard, but it does not simply end with achieving a goal, a good story never ends, because you can tell it over and over again.

GeoTrack is built from the ground up, to help you do exactly that, by not only being an excellent tracker, but to be so much more. Take photos, listen to music and even create advanced training schedules. All in an elegant, easy-to-use and functionality packed app.

Built using the latest technologies for MacOS, iPadOS and iOS to perfectly integrate with your devices and software. GeoTrack is available for iOS 16.0, iPadOS 16.1 and MacOS 13.0 or newer.
